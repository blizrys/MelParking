Parking spaces

The city of Melbourne is a major city that people traverse both for work and pleasure. The chart on the right show an overview of available parking space within the city. Totally, there are 1.2M Parking spaces available.

Our initial observation show that majorly parking space are offer as commercial off-streets. Only 2% (24k) of overall parking spaces are on-street parking which is very limited. This show that the city encourage travelers to use public transport or parking off-street.

Moreover, as the city are pushing on-street parking sensor to become smart city, only 20% (5k) of on-street parking spaces has been implemented.
